Our Philosophy

At the Beauty Parlour, we believe in beauty with a conscience. We have created a salon that offers the highest quality services in a setting that is healthier for the environment, our guests and our staff. The Beauty Parlour is fully furnished to provide the best experience while you wait.

We carry high quality professional products designed to ensure our guests look their best, both in the salon and at home. The beauty care products we carry have been carefully chosen based both on performance and eco-sensitivity.

Our design and philosophy work hand in hand. We are committed to providing healthy care with a low impact on the environment. Come see what you and your personality can do for the Earth.
